1. Introduction to Studying MBBS Abroad

In recent years, pursuing MBBS abroad has become an increasingly popular option for students, particularly for those who want quality education but are unable to secure a spot in their home country’s medical schools. Many countries around the world offer excellent medical programs at a fraction of the cost of studying at private institutions in India or other countries. Universities in countries such as Russia, China, Ukraine, the Philippines, and more have been attracting students due to their affordable fees, high-quality education, and opportunities to practice medicine internationally.
